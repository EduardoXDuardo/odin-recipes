Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 700ml full-fat milk
  • 1 onion (peeled and halved)
  • 1 garlic clove (peeled)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 350g macaroni
  • 50g butter, plus a little extra for greasing
  • 50g plain flour
  • 175g mature cheddar cheese (grated)
  • 1 tsp English mustard
  • 50g parmesan (grated)
  • 50g coarse white breadcrumb
